SSN College Departments

Q: What departments are available in SSN College?
A: SSN College offers departments including Computer Science and Engineering,Information Technology,Electronics and Communication Engineering,Electrical and Electronics Engineering,Mechanical Engineering,Civil Engineering,Biomedical Engineering

Q: Does every department have a Head of Department?
A: Yes, every department has a Head of Department and faculty members.

Q: Who should students contact for academic issues?
A: Students should contact the respective department office for academic related concerns.
