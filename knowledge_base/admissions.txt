SSN College Admissions

Q: How can students apply for admission?
A: Students can apply through TNEA counselling and management quota admissions.

Q: Is management quota admission available?
A: Yes, management quota admissions are available for eligible students.

Q: What documents are required for admission?
A: Students must submit:10th marksheet,12th marksheet,Transfer Certificate,Community Certificate,Passport size photographs

Q: What is the eligibility for engineering admission?
A: Students should complete higher secondary education with Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics.

Q: Who should students contact for admission queries?
A: Students can contact the admissions office for admission related queries.
